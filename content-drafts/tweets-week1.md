### Day 6 ‚Äî Saturday, Feb 23

**Tweet 1 (Thread ‚Äî 5 tweets: "Stop paying for AI tools you don't need")**

1/ I audited my AI subscriptions last month. I was paying $340/mo across 9 tools. I cut it to $95 and ship faster now.

2/ The trap: every new AI tool has a free tier that hooks you, then you upgrade "just for this one feature." Multiply by 10 tools and you're bleeding.

4/ What I kept: Cursor Pro, Claude Pro, one image tool, n8n self-hosted. That's the core. Everything else is optional.
