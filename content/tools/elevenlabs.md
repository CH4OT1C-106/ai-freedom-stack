## What is ElevenLabs?

ElevenLabs is a text-to-speech platform that generates voices nearly indistinguishable from real humans. Upload a script, pick a voice (or clone your own), and get broadcast-quality audio back in seconds.

## Who is it for?

- **Best for:** YouTube creators, podcasters, audiobook producers, anyone who needs voice content at scale
- **Not for:** Live conversation or real-time voice chat applications

**Hidden costs:** Characters burn fast on long-form content. A 10-minute video script uses ~10-15k characters.

**Free tier reality check:** Enough for one short video per month. Not enough for regular content production.

## How we'd actually use it

1. Write your video script (or have AI generate it)
2. Pick a voice from the library that matches your brand
3. Paste the script, generate audio
4. Download and drop into your video editor

**Time saved vs doing it manually:** Recording + editing your own voice: 2-3 hours. ElevenLabs: 5 minutes.
