## What is Midjourney?

An AI image generation tool accessed primarily through Discord. You type prompts, it generates images. The output quality ‚Äî especially for stylized, artistic, and photorealistic content ‚Äî is consistently the best available. V6 is the current model.

## Who is it for?

- **Best for:** Content creators, YouTubers, marketers who need high-quality visuals regularly
- **Not for:** Developers who occasionally need a quick image (use Gemini free), people who hate Discord

## How we'd actually use it

Creating visuals for a YouTube thumbnail and tool review:

1. `/imagine futuristic AI dashboard, dark theme, neon blue accents, clean UI --ar 16:9 --v 6`
2. Pick the best of 4 variations, upscale it
3. `/imagine [tool logo] reimagined as a 3d icon, floating, soft lighting --v 6`
4. Batch generate 5-6 variations for A/B testing thumbnails
5. Download and drop into your editor

**Time saved vs doing it manually:** Hours with a designer or stock photos ‚Üí 15 minutes

## What's good

- Image quality is simply the best ‚Äî nothing else matches the aesthetic consistency

## What's not

- Commercial rights only on paid plans
- No API ‚Äî can't automate into your pipeline easily
- Proprietary and cloud-only ‚Äî total vendor lock-in

## FAQ

**Q: Midjourney or Stable Diffusion?**
A: If you want the best output with minimal setup, Midjourney. If you want free, local, and customizable, Stable Diffusion. Many builders use both.

**Q: Can I use Midjourney images commercially?**
A: Yes, on all paid plans. Read the ToS for specifics on company size limitations.

**Q: Is the web app ready yet?**
A: It's in beta. Still missing features. Discord is the full experience for now.
