## How we'd actually use it

Adding AI video to a YouTube video:

1. Generate a concept clip: "Futuristic AI assistant analyzing data, holographic display, cinematic lighting"
2. Use image-to-video with your existing visuals as starting frames
3. Generate 3-4 five-second clips for key moments
4. Edit into your video alongside screen recordings and static images
5. Total: 4-5 AI clips per video, ~$5-10 worth of credits
