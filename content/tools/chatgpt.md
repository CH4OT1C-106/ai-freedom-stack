## What is ChatGPT?

OpenAI's flagship consumer AI product. GPT-4o is the engine ‚Äî a multimodal model that handles text, images, audio, and code. The app layers on web browsing, DALL-E image generation, file analysis, and a plugin ecosystem. It's the most feature-complete AI assistant available.

## Who is it for?

- **Best for:** Solo builders who want a single AI tool for everything ‚Äî writing, research, coding, images
- **Not for:** People who need the absolute best quality in one domain (Claude beats it for writing, Midjourney for images)

## How we'd actually use it

Building a landing page with all the trimmings:

1. Describe your product to ChatGPT, ask it to draft copy
2. Use DALL-E to generate a hero image
3. Ask it to write the HTML/CSS (or use with Cursor)
4. Have it browse competitor sites and suggest improvements
5. Generate social media posts to promote the launch

## FAQ

**Q: ChatGPT Plus vs Pro ‚Äî is $200/mo ever worth it?**
A: For most solo builders, no. Plus covers 95% of use cases. Pro is for heavy reasoning tasks (o1-pro) that most people don't need.

**Q: Should I use the app or the API?**
A: App for daily work. API when you're building automations or integrating into your own tools.

**Q: Is my data safe with OpenAI?**
A: They've improved, but read the fine print. Turn off "improve our models" in settings. For sensitive work, use the API with data retention off.
