What is Perplexity?

A search engine that uses AI to synthesize real answers from the web, with citations. Ask it anything and it gives you a sourced summary instead of making you click through links. Pro tier adds access to Claude, GPT-4, and deeper analysis.

Who is it for?

  • Not for: People who need creative writing, coding help, or image generation (use Claude/Cursor for those)

FAQ

Q: Do I need Perplexity if I already have ChatGPT? A: Yes. ChatGPT’s browsing is an add-on. Perplexity was built for search from the ground up. The citations alone make it worth it for any research-heavy work.

Q: Can Perplexity replace Google? A: For research and questions, yes. For navigational searches (“take me to gmail”), just use Google. Different tools for different jobs.

Q: Is Pro worth it? A: If you research tools, trends, or competitors regularly, absolutely. The 5 free Pro searches/day is enough to decide.
